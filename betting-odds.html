How To Convert Odds to Implied Probability


Decimal Odds:
Formula: (1 / Decimal Odds) x 100

Example: Converting 5.00 to Implied Probability

• 1 / 5 = 0.20
• 0.20 × 100 = 20%
• Implied Probability: 20%


Fractional Odds:
Formula:  Denominator / (Numerator + Denominator) x 100

Example: Converting 4/1 to Implied Probability
• 1 / (4 + 1) = 1 / 5 = 0.20
• 0.20 × 100 = 20%
• Implied Probability:  20%


American Odds (Positive):
Formula:  100 / (American Odds + 100) x 100

Example: Converting +900 to Implied Probability
• 100 / (900 + 100) = 100 / 1000 = 0.1
• 0.1 × 100 =   10%
• Implied Probability: 10%


American Odds (Negative):
Formula:  |American Odds| / (|American Odds| + 100) x 100

Example: Converting -350 to Implied Probability
• 350 / (350 + 100) = 350 / 450 = 0.7777...
• 0.7777 × 100 =  77.8%
• Implied Probability: 77.8%
