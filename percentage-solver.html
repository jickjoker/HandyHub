Percentage Solver

How to Use

There are 3 input boxes:

Part = the smaller amount

Whole = the total amount

% = the percentage


Enter any 2 known values, and the calculator will automatically solve the missing 3rd value.


Examples:

• 20 is what % of 50?

• 30% of 80 = ?

• 15 is 25% of what number?

Example: 5 is what percentage of 25?


Unknown is (%): 5 ÷ 25 = 0.2


0.2 × 100 = 20%

Example: 20 is 40% of what number?


Unknown is (Whole): 40% = 0.40


20 ÷ 0.40 = 50

Example: 30% of 50 is what number?


Unknown is (Part): 30% = 0.30


50 × 0.30 = 15
