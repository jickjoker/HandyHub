Percentage Solver

How to Use

Enter any two values to find the missing third. The calculator handles the math instantly.


Bonus Feature: The calculator shows the specific arithmetic operation and formula used—perfect for learning the logic behind the numbers.



What to Enter (Choose 2)

  • Part:The specific amount or portion.
  • Whole: The total or original amount.
  • %: The percentage value.


Examples & Formulas

To Find Example Formula
Percentage (%) 5 is what % of 25? 5 ÷ 25 = 0.2 → 0.2 × 100 = 20%
Whole Total 20 is 40% of what? 20 ÷ 0.40 = 50
Part Amount 30% of 50 is what? 50 × 0.30 = 15

How to Solve with the Pyramid Method

  1. Memorise the pyramid image.
  2. Imagine placing your hand over the missing value. This helps you visualize which part is unknown.
  3. Select the correct arithmetic operation.
    Example: If Part is covered, (%) and (Whole) remain, which tells you multiplication is used.
