Percentage Formula Cheat Sheet

1. To find the Part: Part = Whole × (Percentage / 100) Example: 30% of 150? → 150 × 0.3 = 45
2. To find the Percentage: % = (Part ÷ Whole) × 100 Example: 24 is what % of 80? → (24 ÷ 80) × 100 = 30%
3. To find the Whole: Whole = Part ÷ (Percentage / 100) Example: 40 is 80% of what? → 40 ÷ 0.8 = 50


Why Master Percentages?

Percentage problems appear everywhere in daily life. Whether you are calculating retail discounts, checking test grades, or determining tips and budgets, mental math skills give you an advantage.
