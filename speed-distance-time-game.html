Learn to Use the Formula Triangle 🔺

The Speed Distance Time formula triangle is a simple method to quickly determine which arithmetic operation to use.

Try this trick: cover the missing value with your hand. The uncovered parts will reveal which arithmetic operations you need.

How to Use the Triangle

• D (Distance): Multiply S × T
• S (Speed): Divide D ÷ T
• T (Time): Divide D ÷ S

Speed, Distance, and Time Formulas 📝

Speed = Distance ÷ Time
Distance = Speed × Time
Time = Distance ÷ Speed

Why Learn These Formulas?

These formulas help you quickly solve real-world problems involving travel, speed, and time. From exams to everyday situations, understanding them makes calculations faster and easier. And a bit of brain 🧠 training is never a bad thing!
