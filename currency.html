How it works

Select a currency, enter an amount, and view conversion results instantly.


Update frequency:
Live rates refresh automatically every 30 seconds when available. If live data fails, the app switches to local fallback estimates.


Data source:
Exchange rates are retrieved from a public FX API (open.er-api.com). Values may differ slightly from Google or banks due to timing and rounding differences.


Privacy:
This tool does not use cookies or store personal data. All calculations run locally in your browser.
