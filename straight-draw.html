How many outs for a straight?

Straight Draw Trainer


Do you enjoy playing Texas Hold'em poker? Why not improve your poker skills with this free Straight Draw Trainer. This poker training game is designed to help you quickly recognize straight draws, count outs, and improve your flop reading skills.

The game is simple to play. You are dealt a poker hand and a flop, and you must choose one of three possible answers: 0, 4, or 8. The answer represents the number of outs you have to complete a straight on the turn.

Game Modes

1. Practice Mode

The timer is turned off in Practice Mode, allowing you to learn at your own pace. In the top-left corner, there is a counter that tracks your correct answers and the total number of questions played.


2. The 30-Second Straight Challenge

Try to answer 10 questions correctly in a row within 30 seconds to win the gold medal. This mode is designed to improve your speed, pattern recognition, and poker decision-making under pressure.


Number of Outs

Answer = 0

This means there are no cards that can give you a straight on the turn.


Gutshot Straight Draw

Answer = 4

A straight draw with 4 outs is known as a gutshot straight draw, also called an inside straight draw.

Example:

You have:   8♥ 6♣

Flop:   J♣ 7♠ 4♦


There are 4 cards in the deck that will complete your straight:

5♣ 5♦ 5♥ 5♠


Any 5 on the turn will complete the following straight:

8♥ 7♠ 6♣ 5♦ 4♦



Open-Ended Straight Draw (OESD)

Answer = 8

A straight draw with 8 outs is known as an open-ended straight draw (OESD).


Example:

You have:   J♥ 10♦

Flop:   K♥ Q♣ 2♦


There are 8 cards in the deck that will give you a straight.

A♥ A♦ A♣ A♠     or     9♦ 9♥ 9♣ 9♠


An ace or a 9 will complete one of the following straights:

A♦ K♥ Q♣ J♥ 10♦    or    K♥ Q♣ J♥ 10♦ 9♥


Double Belly Buster Straight Draw


A double belly buster straight draw also has 8 outs, but it is harder to recognize than a standard open-ended straight draw.


Example:

You have:   Q♥ 8♦

Flop:   6♦ 10♣ 9♥


There are 8 cards in the deck that will give you a straight.

J♥ J♣ J♦ J♠     or     7♣ 7♥ 7♦ 7♠


A Jack or a 7 will complete one of the following straights:

Q♥ J♦ 10♣ 9♥ 8♦    or     10♣ 9♥ 8♦ 7♦ 6♦
